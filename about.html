About the app...

A easy & secure way to organise all your important documents in one place. Doc Organizer is one of the those application that you may need the most in this digital era. It is a simple & user-friendly app that enables you to organise important documents in a safe and secure environment. Through Doc Organizer you can easily manage and access your documents such as passports, birth certificates, automobile and property records, critical medical information, and/or warranties for expensive home appliances anytime, anywhere.
